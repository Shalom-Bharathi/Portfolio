About this project:

Welcome to our group portfolio! This project marks the culmination of a school assignment, it took over 20 hours of dedicated effort spread across four days to successfully complete this project. Led by Shalom Bharathi, who served as both the Programmer & Animator and Project Manager, our team worked collaboratively to bring this vision to life by spending many sleepless nights working on this project. Puvanesh, Abijan, and Siva served as diligent Researchers, while Laksmana, Sriram, and Muthappa meticulously planned the project as Planners. Sri Ram and Muthappa lent their voices as Speakers, adding depth and clarity to our presentation. Shalom Bharathi's leadership and expertise were instrumental in crafting the entire website, complete with animations and other features. We invite you to explore the unique contributions of each team member as we showcase our collective talents and dedication.
